7. Sensitive Information - Birth Details Protection

🔯 Birth Information Classification: We treat your date, time, and place of birth as HIGHLY SENSITIVE personal data equivalent to biometric information.

⭐ Why Birth Details Are Critical

In astrology, birth details (DoB, ToB, PoB) are the foundation of all calculations. Your complete birth information can potentially be misused if it falls into wrong hands because:

  • Combined with other data, it can enable identity fraud
  • It can be used to create false documentation
  • In certain contexts, it might enable social engineering attacks

🔐 Special Protections for Birth Data

  • Maximum Encryption: Birth details are encrypted at the database level
  • Limited Access: Only assigned astrologers and essential staff can access
  • Restricted Sharing: Birth data is NEVER sold, shared, or used for any purpose other than astrology services
  • Audit Logging: Every access to birth details is logged and reviewed
  • No Third-Party Tools: Birth details are not sent to any external analytics or AI training services
  • Anonymous Operations: Where possible, we process birth data without linking to your identity

❌ Birth Data Restrictions

  • Your birth details will NEVER be used for AI training or machine learning without explicit consent
  • Your birth details will NEVER be shared with insurance, lending, or medical companies
  • Your birth details will NEVER be made available to advertisers or data brokers
  • We will NEVER sell or lease your birth information

  1. You Enter Payment Details: You provide payment information directly on Cashfree's secure page
