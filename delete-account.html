Kalyansevak Jyotish - Account Deletion

This page explains how users can permanently delete their account and personal data from Kalyansevak Jyotish.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Kalyansevak Jyotish mobile application.
  2. Login to your account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Gender
  • Profile Photo
  • Date of Birth
  • Time of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Kundli Requests
  • Matchmaking Requests
  • Chat and Consultation Data
  • Device Tokens and Preferences
Personal account information is permanently removed after successful account deletion.

Data That May Be Retained

  • Payment transaction records
  • Order identifiers
  • Tax and accounting records
Certain payment and transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with legal, tax, fraud prevention, and accounting requirements.
