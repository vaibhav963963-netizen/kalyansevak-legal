Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026 Effective Date: June 27, 2026 Version: 1.0
Data Privacy and Rights Control: Google Play Store policy requires apps that support account creation to allow users to request account deletion. At Shree KalyanSevak Jyotish, we give you full control over your personal data. Below are instructions on how to permanently delete your account and what happens to your data.

1. Steps to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and associated personal data directly from our mobile application or by submitting an email request.

Option A: In-App Deletion (Recommended & Instant)

Follow these steps inside the app on your mobile device:

1

Open App

Launch the Kalyansevak Jyotish App and make sure you are logged in.

2

Navigate to Profile

Tap on your profile tab or open the Settings menu.

3
3

Tap Delete Account

Locate the "Delete Account" option in the security or privacy settings.

4

Confirm Request

Review the warning prompt detailing the deletion. Tap "Confirm Delete" to execute.

Option B: Email Deletion Request

If you have uninstalled the App or are unable to log in, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  • Send an email to: vishwtejkulkarni@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Provide your registered Phone Number and Full Name so we can verify your identity.
  • We will process your request and permanently delete your account within 30 days.

2. What Personal Data is Permanently Deleted

Upon deletion, the following data is permanently wiped from our active servers within 30 days (and removed from historical secure backup vaults within 90 days):

Profile & Credentials

  • Full Name & Gender
  • Registered Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Profile Photo
  • Account Password hashes

Astrological Records

  • Birth Details (DoB, ToB, PoB)
  • Generated Kundli Chart files
  • Matchmaking compatibility reports
  • Consultation logs and chat logs
  • Messages with Astrologers

3. What Data is Retained (Legal Obligation)

To comply with regulatory, tax, accounting, and anti-fraud guidelines, some transaction data cannot be immediately deleted:

Retained Billing Information: Under the Indian Income Tax Act and GST regulations, transaction records, tax invoices, refund logs, and payment settlements (via **Cashfree**) must be archived for a period of up to 7 years. This archived data is stored in isolated, encrypted tables, is never accessed for service purposes, and will be automatically purged after the legal retention window expires.

4. Reversing Account Deletion

Please note that account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, we cannot recover your consultations, charts, or payment balance. If you wish to use our services again in the future, you will need to register as a new user and create a new account.
