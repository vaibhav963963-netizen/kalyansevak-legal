2. Non-Refundable Scenarios

We do not issue refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Incorrect Birth Details: If you input incorrect Date of Birth, Time of Birth, or Place of Birth, the calculations will be incorrect. We do not refund reports generated using user-provided incorrect information. (However, you can contact support to update details and regenerate reports for free).
  • Disagreement with Predictions: Astrology is guidance. We do not offer refunds simply because a prediction did not come true or you do not agree with an astrologer's spiritual guidance.
  • Expired Bookings: Live consultation minutes that have been consumed cannot be refunded.
  • Platform Violations: Accounts terminated due to a violation of our Terms & Conditions are not eligible for refunds.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Prepare an email to: vishwtejkulkarni@gmail.com
  2. Set the subject line as: Refund Request - Order #[Your Order ID]
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • Your registered Phone Number and Full Name
    • The Date of Transaction & Amount Paid
    • The specific service purchased
    • A brief description of the reason for your refund request (e.g., "Astrologer did not join", "Incorrect billing", "Unsatisfied with Report quality")

4. Processing Timelines & Transaction Fees

Once we receive your refund request, it will be evaluated by our support team within 2 to 3 business days.

  • If approved, the refund will be initiated immediately.
  • Refunds are processed back to the **original payment method** used at the time of purchase via our payment gateway, Cashfree.
  • Settlement Timeline: The refunded amount typically reflects in your bank account, credit card statement, or UPI wallet within 7 to 10 business days, depending on your bank's processing cycles.
  • Please note that transaction fees charged by Cashfree or currency conversion charges (if any) are non-refundable and may be deducted from the final refund amount.
